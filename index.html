Our Team


Johan van Gaalen, Owner and Director of Van Gaalen Attorneys, has acquired extensive knowledge and experience in Labour, Corporate, and Conveyancing matters. While representing several major corporations with their Labour and Commercial disputes, Johan is also actively involved in the development and elevation of Sports Law throughout South Africa.

In addition to serving as an Arbitrator on the FIFA Dispute Resolution Chamber for the past 12 years, he is also a Single Judge and a Member of the FIFA Players Status Committee for 9 years running. Johan has been elected by the International Who’s Who of Sport & Entertainment Lawyers as being amongst the world’s leading Sports and Entertainment Lawyers. He is also a Scientific Board Member of the African Sports Law Journal and Co-founder of the Centre of Sports Law at the University of Johannesburg.
