Our Team

Johan van Gaalen, Owner and Director of Van Gaalen Attorneys, has acquired extensive knowledge and experience in Labour, Corporate and Conveyancing matters. Elected by the International Who’s Who of Sport & Entertainment Lawyers, Johan has been among the world’s leading Sports and Entertainment Lawyers from 2013 to 2020.

In addition to serving as an Arbitrator on the FIFA Dispute Resolution Chamber for the past 12 years, he is also a Single Judge and a member of the FIFA Players Status Committee for 9 years running.
